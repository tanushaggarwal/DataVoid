# PrivacyBlitz Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

PrivacyBlitz ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the PrivacyBlitz Chrome extension.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

PrivacyBlitz processes data **locally on your device only**. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The extension accesses the following data types:

1. **Browser History**: To scan for account-related pages (login/signup pages)
2. **Cookies**: To identify sites with authentication data
3. **Storage Data**: Scan results and settings stored locally using Chrome's storage API

### What We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT transmit data to external servers
- We do NOT track your browsing behavior
- We do NOT share data with third parties
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT access your passwords or sensitive credentials

## How We Use Your Data

All data processing happens **locally in your browser**:

1. **Account Detection**: We scan your browser history to identify potential account pages
2. **Local Storage**: Scan results are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
3. **Settings**: Your preferences are stored locally and synced across your devices (if Chrome sync is enabled)

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` and `chrome.storage.sync` APIs
- Scan results are stored on your device only
- Settings may be synced across your devices if you have Chrome sync enabled
- You can clear all data at any time through the extension's options page

## Permissions

PrivacyBlitz requires the following permissions:

- **`storage`**: To save scan results and settings locally
- **`history`**: To scan browser history for account-related pages
- **`tabs`**: To open account deletion guides in new tabs
- **`activeTab`**: To detect account pages on the current tab
- **`cookies`**: To identify sites with authentication cookies
- **`<all_urls>`**: To scan history across all websites

These permissions are necessary for the extension's core functionality. We do not use these permissions for any other purpose.

## Third-Party Services

PrivacyBlitz does not integrate with any third-party services. All processing happens locally in your browser.

## Data Security

- All data processing occurs locally on your device
- No data is transmitted over the network
- We use Chrome's built-in security features
- You have full control over your data

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

1. **Access**: View all stored data through the extension's options page
2. **Export**: Export your scan results and settings as JSON
3. **Delete**: Clear all data at any time through the options page
4. **Control**: Disable or uninstall the extension at any time

## Children's Privacy

PrivacyBlitz is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through:
- GitHub Issues (if open source)
- Chrome Web Store support page

## Compliance

PrivacyBlitz is designed to comply with:
- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **Chrome Web Store Policies**

## Data Retention

- Scan results are retained according to your settings (default: 30 days)
- You can configure data retention in the extension's options page
- You can manually delete data at any time

PrivacyBlitz is open source. You can review the source code to verify our privacy practices.
