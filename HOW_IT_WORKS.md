### Why Can't It Auto-Delete?

Chrome extensions **cannot** automatically delete accounts because:

1. **Security**: Auto-deleting would require storing your passwords, which is a major security risk
2. **Authentication**: Each website has different login systems and deletion processes
3. **Confirmation**: Most sites require you to confirm deletion yourself
4. **Legal**: Automatic deletion could violate terms of service
